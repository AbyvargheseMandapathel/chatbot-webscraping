Welcome

CEK (College of Engineering, Kidangoor) in Kottayam is a premier institution governed by the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education established by the Government of Kerala. Admissions are based on state entrance examination rankings, and the college operates according to Government of Kerala regulations. CEK boasts excellent academic and extracurricular records, thanks to outstanding faculty and facilities.
